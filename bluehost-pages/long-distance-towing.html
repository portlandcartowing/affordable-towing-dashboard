If you need to move a vehicle far beyond the Portland metro — a move out of state, a breakdown hours from home, a car purchased at auction, or a non-running vehicle that needs to reach a specialty shop — Affordable Car Towing LLC handles long-distance tow jobs across the Pacific Northwest. FAQ

Long-distance towing frequently asked questions

How far will you tow a vehicle from Portland?

We tow long-distance throughout Oregon, Washington, Idaho, and Northern California. Common routes include Portland to Seattle, Eugene, Medford, Bend, Boise, Spokane, and Sacramento. For moves beyond 800 miles, call us for a custom quote.

How much does long distance towing cost?

Long-distance towing is priced per mile with a trip minimum. Rates depend on total distance, vehicle size, and pickup conditions. Call (503) 608-7014 with your pickup and drop-off locations for an upfront quote — no hidden fees.

Do you use a flatbed for long distance tows?

Yes. For any tow over about 30 miles we use a flatbed to keep all four wheels off the road. This protects the drivetrain, tires, and suspension over long distances and is required for AWD, 4WD, and lowered vehicles.

Can you tow a non-running vehicle long distance?

Yes. We regularly move non-running, accident-damaged, or project vehicles long-distance. Our flatbed winch loads vehicles that cannot roll, and we secure them for safe interstate transport.

Can you tow a car across state lines?

Yes. We provide interstate towing from Oregon into Washington, Idaho, and California. We are fully licensed and insured for out-of-state vehicle transport. Call for a quote on your specific route.

Do I need to be present for pickup and drop-off?

Not always. If someone else can hand off the keys at pickup and another person can receive the vehicle at drop-off, we can handle the transport without you being present at either end. We confirm details before dispatch.

How soon can you pick up for a long distance tow?

Same-day pickup is usually available in the Portland metro depending on driver availability. For scheduled long-distance moves we recommend booking 24 to 48 hours in advance to reserve the right equipment.
